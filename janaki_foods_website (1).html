About Us

Janaki Foods brings you the rich taste of Bengal with every bite. Rooted in tradition and prepared with love, our meals are inspired by generations of home-cooked Bengali recipes — from spicy maacher jhol to mouthwatering kosha mangsho.

  • Nolen Gur Sandesh – Seasonal Bengali sweet
